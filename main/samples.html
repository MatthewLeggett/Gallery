This song portrays the densest known material is the metallic element osmium, which packs 22 grams into 1 cubic centimetre.

This song portrays a dwarf planet in the Kuiper belt, a ring of bodies beyond the orbit of Neptune. It was the first and the largest Kuiper belt object to be discovered.
